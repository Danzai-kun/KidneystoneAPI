This AI-powered system helps detect kidney stones from CT scan images using deep learning models. It is designed to assist medical professionals and patients in identifying early signs of kidney stones quickly and accurately.

What are Kidney stones?

Kidney stones are hard deposits made of minerals and salts that form inside your kidneys. They develop when your urine contains more crystal-forming substances (like calcium, oxalate, and uric acid) than the fluid in your urine can dilute.

  • Solid crystals formed from minerals in urine
  • Can cause pain, nausea, and difficulty urinating
  • Prevention: Drink plenty of water daily
