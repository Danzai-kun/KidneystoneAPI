About Our System

The Kidney Stone Prediction System is a cutting-edge diagnostic tool that leverages deep learning algorithms to analyze CT and MRI scans for signs of kidney stones. It was developed to assist healthcare professionals and patients in early diagnosis.

Our mission is to make healthcare smarter and more accessible by integrating AI into routine diagnostics, improving speed, accuracy, and patient outcomes.
